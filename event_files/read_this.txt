Normally, the event_files folder would contain the following:

bevent.exe;
bgame.exe;
box.exe;
Event files ending in .EVN or .EVA;
Roster files ending in .ROS;
Team files of the form TEAM2000, TEAM2001, ...

These resources are necessary to get the project to work.

Anyone interested in obtaining these resources can visit retrosheet's website at https://www.retrosheet.org/ .
